Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is located in West Africa. It has a tropical climate and is known for its natural harbor and rich mineral resources like diamonds, rutile, and bauxite. Its capital, Freetown, was founded for freed slaves. The country features rainforests, mountains, and savannas.
