Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is located in West Africa. Sierra Leone is a tropical country in West Africa, known for its large natural harbor and mineral resources like diamonds, rutile, and bauxite, which form its economic base. Its capital is Freetown, established as a resettlement site for freed slaves, and the country has a diverse geography of rainforests, mountains, and savanna
