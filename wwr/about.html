White Water Rafting company

Purpose

To ensure access to safe, reliable, and affordable water and sanitation services for communities across Sierra Leone, fostering public health and sustainable development.

Mission

To provide reliable and affordable quality water supply and sanitation services in a cost-effective and environmentally sustainable manner to the satisfaction of our customers

Creed

We uphold professionalism, integrity, and customer-centric service delivery. Our commitment to reliability, efficiency, and dedication drives our mission to serve the people of Sierra Leone.

Motto

"Enhancing Lives Through Clean Water"

History

Founded in the year of the great water whisper, SALWACO emerged from the bubbling springs of innovation and the echoing calls of thirsty townsfolk. With pipes forged from moonlight and valves tuned to the rhythm of the rainforest, the company began its journey to hydrate the heart of Sierra Leone. Over the decades, it has grown from a humble puddle to a mighty river of service and sustainability.
